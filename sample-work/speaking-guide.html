üéØ Part 1: Common Questions & Band 5.0-6.5 Answers

1. What is your favourite room?
Band 5.0 Answer
"My favourite room is my bedroom. I like it because it's quiet and I can relax there. I have my computer and books in my bedroom. I spend a lot of time there."

Band 6.0 Answer
"I think my favourite room is definitely my bedroom. The main reason is that it's my personal space where I can relax after a long day. I've decorated it with some posters and photos, which makes it feel comfortable. Also, I have a desk there where I can study without being disturbed."

Key phrases to use: ‚Ä¢ "I think..." / "I believe..." ‚Ä¢ "The main reason is..." ‚Ä¢ "Also..." / "Besides..."
2. Do you live in a house or a flat?
Band 5.0 Answer
"I live in a flat. It's on the third floor. We have two bedrooms, a living room and a kitchen. It's not very big but it's enough for my family."

Band 6.0 Answer
"Actually, I live in a flat in an apartment building. It's on the third floor, which is nice because it's not too high but we still get good sunlight. Although it's not very spacious, it has two bedrooms and a comfortable living room, which is sufficient for my family of three."

Useful connectors: ‚Ä¢ "Actually..." (to start) ‚Ä¢ "Although..." (for contrast) ‚Ä¢ "...which is..." (to add information)
3. Who do you live with?
Band 5.0 Answer
"I live with my parents and my younger sister. We have lived together for many years. Sometimes it's noisy but I like living with my family."

Band 6.0 Answer
"Currently, I'm living with my parents and my younger sister. We've been living together in the same flat for about five years now. To be honest, sometimes I wish I had more privacy, but I enjoy the family atmosphere and it's nice to have someone to talk to when I come home."

Time expressions: ‚Ä¢ "Currently..." / "At the moment..." ‚Ä¢ "We've been living... for..." ‚Ä¢ "To be honest..."

üéØ Part 2: Cue Card Practice (Band 5.0-6.0)

Describe the house or apartment where you live

You should say:

  • What type of accommodation it is
  • Who you live with
  • What your favorite room is
  • And explain what you like or dislike about it

Preparation: 1 minute Speaking: 1-2 minutes
Sample Answer (Band 5.0-6.0):

Complete answer:
"I'd like to talk about my current home, which is an apartment in the city center. I live in a two-bedroom apartment on the fifth floor of a modern building. It's not very large, about 70 square meters, but it's comfortable for our family. The building has an elevator and a small parking area. I share this apartment with my parents and my younger brother. We've been living here together for about four years. Sometimes it feels a bit crowded, especially in the morning when everyone is getting ready, but generally we manage well. My favorite room is definitely my bedroom, even though I share it with my brother. We have our own desks and I've decorated my side with some posters. It's the place where I can study and relax. What I really like about our apartment is the location - it's very convenient for shops and public transport. Also, we have nice neighbors who are friendly and helpful. However, one thing I don't like is that it can be quite noisy because we're near a main road. Sometimes it's hard to concentrate when studying."
